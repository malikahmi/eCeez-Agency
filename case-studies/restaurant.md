# Case Study: Digital Menu Architecture and Local organic Acquisition Campaign

> How eCeez restructured the digital footprint of a multi-location gourmet restaurant chain, integrating Menu schema validation and capturing +160% online takeout volume.

## Client Profile
- **Industry**: Premium Gourmet Culinary & Multi-unit Restaurant Services
- **Primary Challenges**: Invisible menu search crawls, clunky pdf-based dish layouts on mobile, slower reservation site speeds.

---

## Technical Solutions Implemented

### 1. Restaurant Menu JSON-LD Schema
PDF menus are invisible to search engines. We coded a responsive schema-rich digital menu:
- Created structured lists utilizing `MenuSection` and `MenuItem` schemas, detailing precise ingredient profiles, allergy indicators, and localized pricing.
- This allowed Google to render the restaurant's dishes directly in rich localized search tabs.

### 2. Accelerated Mobile Ordering Page Speed
- Built an ordering frontend using optimized static-hydration modules.
- Lazy-loaded high-contrast imagery of dishes using next-gen formats with pre-rendered cache structures.

## Notable Takeaways
1. **Ditch Menu PDFs**: Search algorithms cannot parse or recommend food options stored strictly inside image PDFs. Dynamic, structured HTML5 menus are the industry gold standard.
2. **Order Accessibility**: Instant action buttons paired directly in Google My Business listings and maps widgets drive immediate buyer action.
